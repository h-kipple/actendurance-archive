Photos

National Capital Brookvale 30th anniversary ride – 28 October 2012

Andrew Hennell photographed horses and riders in action on the course, mostly on the second leg; some of his photos are included here. Roz Edmunds captured scenes of the new second leg prior to the ride, and at the presentations. The photos provided by Uros Rokvic and Kim Stuart record the atmosphere and activity at the ride base during the weekend and also include the participants in the 1982 ride who returned for the anniversary celebrations. All photos are copyright to their respective photographer.
