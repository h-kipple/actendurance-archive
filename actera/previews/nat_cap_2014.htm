National Capital Brookvale, 18–19 October 2014

As is traditional at this time of year, the ACT Endurance Riders Association (ACTERA) would like to invite you to join them for the Brookvale ride near Canberra on the weekend of 18 & 19 October 2014. Rides of 80 km, 40 km and 20 km distance will be held on the Sunday.

Come and enjoy the friendly atmosphere and bush scenery the Brookvale ride offers. Volunteers are welcome! The ride is affiliated with the Snowy Zone and counts towards the zone point scores.

The course

The 80 km ride consists of two legs of 50 km and 30 km distance respectively.

The first leg travels through Brindabella National Park, among other trails utilising the beautiful Webb's Ridge Fire Trail, returning to the ride base via Two Sticks Road.

The second leg winds through the Uriarra Pine Forest, where we found a new loop with the most fantastic views of surrounding areas, and returns to base via private property.

See photos: map of 50 km first leg | map of 30 km second leg | view from second leg | another view from second leg | further view from second leg

The 40 km course initially follows the second leg of the 80 km course, but then veers off to follow Blue Range Road to the top of the range, again providing stunning views towards Canberra and surrounds. See map of 40 km course.

The 22 km introductory ride is an abbreviated version of the second leg of the 80 km ride and follows an undulating course in Uriarra Forest. See map of 22 km introductory ride.

The footing for all rides is a mixture of forest tracks and roads, rocky stretches and dirt road. Both the 80 km and 40 km rides are true challenging mountain rides and therefore require horses that are fit for the respective ride distance. See photo of track footing at start of new loop..

Admin and vetting

Office and vetting are open 12.00 pm–5.00 pm on Saturday.

We encourage riders in the 40 km and 20 km rides to vet on Saturday, but those who don't wish to camp overnight may vet on Sunday from 7.30 am. Please arrive no later than 7.30 am on Sunday morning to allow enough time to enter and vet before your ride.

Ride base

Brookvale is a no-frills campsite in a picturesque valley with a freshwater creek running through it. Facilities are basic, with bush toilets/portaloos and horse water available. You will need to bring your own drinking water.

The possibility of lighting enclosed cooking fires will depend on the development of the fire season.

Important: Brookvale is a working sheep and cattle property. If you can, please don't bring dogs to the ride, but if you need to, they must be kept on a lead at all times.

Catering

A canteen will be open from Saturday lunch until Sunday afternoon, serving sausage sandwiches, egg & bacon sandwiches, burgers, salad, cakes and muffins, as well as hot and cold drinks.

The closest shops and petrol station are located at the Cooleman Court Shopping Centre in Weston Creek, approximately half an hour from the ride base.

Directions

The entry to the Brookvale property is located off Mountain Creek Road, approximately 3.8 km past the intersection of Uriarra Road, ACT, and Mountain Creek Road. The ride base is approximately 1.5 km along Doctors Flat Road, which commences at the property entry. Signs to the ride base will be put up from Uriarra Road onwards when coming from Canberra and at the property entry when approaching along Mountain Creek Road from the Yass/Wee Jasper end.

Further details

Please check this website for information and updates closer to the ride date, and the NSWERA website for a link to Google Maps showing the ride base coordinates. Look for Canberra - Brookvale in the list on the Google Maps page.

Prenomination

Please prenominate by midnight Sunday 12 October. Either use the form on the NSWERA website for this purpose or email Gertraud Norton or phone 02 6227 1378 (AH) or 0427 617 114.

We look forward to seeing you at Brookvale for a great weekend!
