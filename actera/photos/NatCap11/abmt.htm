National Capital Brookvale 2011

Allen Blundell Memorial Trophy


Judy Blundell presents the Allen Blundell Memorial Trophy to Peter Norman as the 'Best Managed' horse and rider team at the 80 km Brookvale ride on Sunday 11 September 2011.


The inaugral Allen Blundell Memorial Trophy presented this year to Peter Norman and Danjera Montana.

The trophy recognises the generous support of the late Allen Blundell, whose family have kindly supported the ACT Endurance Riders Association by allowing us to host our ride on their property at Brookvale each year since 1982.
