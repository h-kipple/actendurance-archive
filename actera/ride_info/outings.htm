Ride outings around the ACT

ACTERA members ride in August

ACTERA is hosting a series of 'ride outings' for those interested in learning about endurance and training with other endurance riders.

This is an affiliated club activity, and a perfect way to meet other members and to train your horse in the lead up to the Woodstock Ride (120/80/40/20 km) in September and ACTERA's National Capital Brookvale ride (80/40/22 km) in October. See a preview of that ride.

When

Saturday 16 August, 10.30 am. You can arrive any time after 8.30 am.

Where

Equestrian Park, Cotter Road, Curtin. Parking is available on the main driveway at Equestrian Park. We will meet close to the canteen. See a Google map on the NSWERA website for how to get to Equestrian Park (click on 'Canberra – Equestrian Park Curtin' in the list at left of screen).

What to bring

Your horse and horse gear. As per rules of the Australian Endurance Riders Association (AERA), all riders must wear an approved standard helmet. NO whips or spurs. Please bring your own food and drink.

Cost

Free to ACTERA members with NSWERA riding membership. Please note, this event is for ACTERA members only. You are welcome to join ACTERA on the day or beforehand – see details on this website about joining. Costs on the day for non-ACTERA members will be:

  • $5.00 for NSWERA day membership if required (for public liability insurance)
  • ACTERA joining fee $15.00 single, $20.00 family per year.

Rides available

There will be two rides to choose from:

  • 7–10 km for beginners
  • 25 km for the more advanced.

How to join in

Contact Fia on 02 6282 2051 or 0401 999 473. Email: Fia.Cumming@health.gov.au. RSVP preferred.

Non riders are welcome. Dogs on a lead if you absolutely must bring them.

If you have a spare float bay, please let us know. If you need a float bay, please ask (see contact details above).
