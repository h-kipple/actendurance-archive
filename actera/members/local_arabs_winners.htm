Horsing around

Local Arabs steal the show

At the National Capital Horse Show on 29 November 2008, some well-known local owners and their endurance Arabs did themselves proud. The horses between them took out first place, champion and reserve champion in the classes for pure/derivative Arabians in endurance or race training.


Belinda Hopley and Sos (Gulargimbone Sorcerer) – 1st gelding

 


Brian Walsh and Tilly (Tingaringi Tilly) – 1st mare & Reserve Champion

Kristen Proudfoot and Searle Johnson's stallion, M'nyana, shown by Wendy Eddey – 1st stallion & Champion

Progeny of M'nyana are now available for sale, including a 3 yo gelding just started under saddle. Phone Kristen or Searle on (02) 4848 0173.
