ACT Endurance Riders Association

National Capital 'Brookvale' Ride

10 - 11 October 2020

The ACT Endurance Riders Association will host an 80km, along with 53km (40km + 13km) Elevator Ride, 40km, 20km and 13km rides on Sunday 11 October at 'Brookvale' on the outskirts of Canberra (subject to COVID-19 restrictions).

Download the ride flyer and find out more at the ACT Endurance Riders Facebook page.
